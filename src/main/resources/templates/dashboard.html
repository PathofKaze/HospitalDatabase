New medical social worker

Let us welcome Destiny Burke into our MSW team!, May 20, 2022

Destiny came to Singapore under a work visa in 2010 to work as a nurse in the Physiotherapy ward of Raffles Hospital. She specialises in palliative care as she graduated with a degree in Rehabilitation Studies and Service from the University of Arizona, United States. Now, having shifted to medical social work, she aims to provide out of hospital care to patients that are in need of monitoring and care


Change in taxi reimbursements

Decrease in claimable amount for taxi fares, May 05, 2022

As we will be providing work transport for medical social workers that need to go down on site to visit their patients, we will be reducing the monthly claimable amount for taxi fares by our nurses by $100. Hence, the new claimable amount will be $200. Please seek HR for further clarifications when in doubt, but the procedure for claims remains the same.
